Department of MCA (Master of Computer Applications) was established in the year 1998 approved by AICTE New Delhi and affiliated to VTU Belgum having present intake of 60 students. The department is well equipped to meet the needs of the present market requirements.

The objectives of MCA Dept is to make the students to work with cutting edge technologies so that they can adopt to the present dynamic IT world. Our mission is to work with motto "Friend, Philosopher and Guide" to bring out the inherent talents of each student, identify their weakness and guide them to build their personality with the aim of making them better citizen of the tomorrow.

Features
  • Well furnished, fully equipped computer laboratories.
  • Softwares such as UNIX, Windows XP, Linux, VB, Java,Oracle Server, Java Web Server, C, C++, COBOL etc., are available.
  • High speed broad band internet connectivity is available.
  • Students are participating in extra curricular activities conducted by the department & college.
  • Separate library with 2550 Volumes and 545 Titles.
  • Many staff have presented paper at national level seminars/conferences.
  • Students are encouraged to deliver the seminars on current topics in every semester.
  • Department organizes extra curricular activity for overall development of staff & student.
